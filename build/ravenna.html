The Club at Ravenna website

Old World Charm, New World Luxury

The Club at Ravenna is a 634-acre private golf and resort lifestyle community located in Southwest, Colorado. With such an expansive property and high-class amenities, Ravenna needed a site to highlight it. This site features drone video of the property, and high-quality imagery* to give viewers a taste of what The Club at Ravenna’s about.

For the website, my team at Clubessential and I decided to showcase the media first and foremost. In addition, we integrated an interactive property map for prospective residents to get a better sense of the area.

*Image and video Media provided by client.
