Release app design for HackOTR

Short Term Leasing Solution

Release is a concept app created during HackOTR 2015, a hackathon sponsored by the Brandery. Given no limitations on ideas, we decided to tackle student housing & subleasing. Our main focus was to develop an app that could provide short-term housing and subletting solutions for students that travel on co-ops and internships. In 24 hours, my team and I created a brand identity, user flow, and working prototype of the app.
